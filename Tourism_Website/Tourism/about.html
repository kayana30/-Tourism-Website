About Us

Tourism has long been the country's third-largest source of foreign currency. The tropical climate, beautiful beaches, and incredible food of Sri Lanka are just some of the many reasons to include the South Asian country on your travel wish list.

In this island nation known as the Pearl of the Indian Ocean, you can explore ancient history by visiting the Sigiriya rock fortress, witness a giant gathering of Asian elephants in Minneriya National Park, or surf at Arugam Bay or Hikkaduwa.

Sri Lanka is a paradise for water sports lovers, wildlife enthusiasts, and history buffs. We provide insights into the best places to visit, as well as accommodations to help you plan your trip.

About Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka has been steadily rebuilding its tourism, offering world-class experiences while preserving its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty.
